UTM URL Generator

UTM tags help you track the performance of your marketing campaigns. By appending parameters ?utm_source=, utm_medium= and utm_campaign= to any URL, Google Analytics (or any analytics tool) will tell you exactly where your traffic came from.

How to use

  1. Paste your base URL into the Base URL field.
  2. Fill in UTM Source, Medium and Campaign (optional: Term, Content).
  3. Click Generate URL and copy your new trackable link.

Why QuickTools UTM Generator?

  • 100% client-side: no login, no data is stored on our servers.
  • Instant encoding ensures URLs are safe and correct.
  • Works offline after PWA installation.
