Strong Password Generator

A secure password is your first line of defense against unauthorized access. QuickTools Strong Password Generator creates random, high-entropy passwords of your chosen length, including options for lowercase, uppercase, numbers, and symbols.

Key Advantages

  • Truly random: uses a cryptographically secure random source for each character.
  • Customizable: choose length from 4 to 32 characters and include any character sets.
  • Instant copy: one click to copy the generated password to your clipboard.
  • Client-side only: no server calls, your passwords never leave your device.

How to Use

  1. Move the Length slider to set your desired password size.
  2. Check boxes for Lowercase, Uppercase, Numbers, and Symbols.
  3. Click Generate to produce your new password.
  4. Click Copy to store it in your clipboard.

Why It Matters

Weak or reused passwords are the leading cause of data breaches. A password of at least 12 characters that mixes multiple character types massively increases protection against brute-force and dictionary attacks.

Best Practices

  • Use unique passwords for every account.
  • Store passwords in a trusted password manager.
  • Rotate sensitive passwords regularly.
